How The Bakers was Born

"The journey of TB began with a simple love for baking. Growing up, I was always fascinated by the art of creating delightful pastries, cakes, and bread. Over time, my passion grew, and I decided to turn it into something bigger.

It all started in my home kitchen, experimenting with recipes and perfecting each delicacy. Eventually, what started as a hobby became a small business. With the support of my family and friends, I was able to launch TB, a place where quality and passion meet in every bite.

Today, TB is more than just a business—it's a dream that turned into reality, and a testament to the power of following your passion".

Our Prestigious Award

We are honored to have received recognition from the Dubai Government for our excellence in the bakery industry.
